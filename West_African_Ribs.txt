	
	[West African Ribs]
	
	Ingredients Needed:
      2 racks pork back ribs
      1 onion (chopped)
      2 cloves garlic (minced)
      2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
      1 Tbsp. coriander
      2 Tbsp. lemon juice
      2 tsp. red pepper flakes
      1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
      salt
      pepper

  DIRECTIONS:

  1.In blender container blend all ingredients except ribs until smooth. Spread paste evenly on ribs.
  2.Roast in shallow pan in 350 degree F. oven for 1 1/2 hours until ribs are tender.
  3.Done!
